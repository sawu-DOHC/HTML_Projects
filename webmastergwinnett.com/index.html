How It Works

  1. Step 1: Secure your domain
    You purchase a domain (usually around $12 per year) from a registrar like GoDaddy or DomainCheap. It’s registered in your name, so you fully own it. I’ll walk you through the setup.
    ⚠️ If someone else buys the domain for you, they legally own it—not you. A lot of web design firms do this to lock you in. If you ever cancel their service, they keep the domain, like yourbusiness.com. You might be able to buy it back, but it won’t cost twelve dollars.

  2. Step 2: I build your site
    I hand code everything from scratch. It's fast, mobile friendly, and optimized for search engines. No templates, no fluff. When I'm done, you pay for it—and you own it. You'll get all your site files plus clear documentation on how it works.

  3. Step 3: You launch and own it
    Once it’s live, it’s yours. You can grow it, manage it, have me manage it, hand it off, or ditch it completely. No restrictions, no strings. Just like when you buy something at the store
