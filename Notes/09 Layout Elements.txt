9. Layout Elements:

<div>: A block-level container used to group content for styling or scripting purposes.

<span>: An inline container for styling or grouping text.

<header>: Defines the header section of a page or a section (often contains logos, navigation, etc.).

<footer>: Defines the footer section of a page or a section (often contains copyright information, contact info, etc.).

<section>: Defines a section in the document (usually with a heading).

<article>: Represents a self-contained piece of content (like a blog post or news article).
    
<nav>: Defines a navigation block, typically for links or menus.
